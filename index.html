Prologue: Earth in Crisis

In the second half of the 21st century, a sharp decline in honeybee populations caused a major crisis in the Earth's ecosystem. When the bees disappeared, food production plummeted, and the flowers withered unmodified. Humans realized the seriousness of the problem, but it was difficult to find a way to solve it. Neo that emerged then—a young innovator who uses AI technology to restore balance between the ecosystem and the economy.

Chapter 1: Taking crisis as an opportunity

Neo has long been interested in honey bee conservation and ecosystem restoration. He plans a blockchain-based cryptocurrency project called Bee Token. HONEY BEE TOKEN is a system that provides HONEY TOKEN rewards to those who have contributed to honey bee conservation, an ecosystem project that generates economic revenue in conjunction with the AI beekeeping and hornet repellent drone operation.

  • AI apiary: It monitors honeybee communities in real time and automatically adjusts optimum temperature and humidity to help honeybees reproduce.
  • Wal eradication drones: AI-powered drones detect and quickly remove bees' greatest natural enemies, increasing their survival rate.

Chapter 2: Blockchain and Ecosystem Connection

Neo introduces HONEY Token to give economic value to activities protecting the ecosystem.

  • HONEY TOKEN WILL BE ACCOUNTED FOR HONEY PROTECTIVE PARTICIPANT: HONEY TOKEN WILL BE ACCOUNTED TO REMOVE WASP OR CONTRIBUTE HONEY COLLEGE.
  • Environmental Restoration Project Funding: Investors can fund ecological restoration projects and split profits through HONEY BEE, HONEY TOKEN.

Chapter 3: War on Wasps

However, while the project is going smoothly, a powerful variant of wasp called Super Wasp appears. With regular drones becoming difficult to respond to, Neo upgrades its AI technology to develop Autonomous Combat Drone. Drones are systems that precisely track and eliminate super wasps without harming bees, and the survival rate of honeybee colonies is remarkably improved.

Chapter 4: Revival of Ecosystems

The effect of AI beekeeping and hornet repellent drones increases the number of honeybees again, and the ecosystem gradually begins to recover. Neo's project draws worldwide attention and establishes itself as a new model of ecological protection and economic success. HONEY BEE Token has become loved by many as an eco-friendly coin.
